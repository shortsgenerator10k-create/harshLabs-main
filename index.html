02

Check your scheme on file

These are the standard published requirements. Click a tab — it's the actual checklist, not a sales pitch.

PMEGP — Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme

Implemented by KVIC · subsidy-linked credit, not a grant
Eligibility
  • New manufacturing unit, up to ₹50L project cost
  • New service unit, up to ₹20L project cost
  • Promoter contribution: 5% (special category) to 10% (general)
Documents we'd prepare
  • Project Report with cost breakup
  • Identity, address, education proof
  • Caste/category certificate if applicable
  • EDP training confirmation (post-sanction)
Subsidy is sanctioned by KVIC/bank after appraisal — typically 15–35% depending on category and area. We cannot set or promise this number for you.

PM MUDRA Yojana

Shishu / Kishore / Tarun tiers · collateral-free up to ₹10L
Eligibility
  • Non-corporate, non-farm micro/small enterprise
  • No strict minimum vintage for Shishu tier
  • Available via banks, RRBs, NBFCs, MFIs
Documents we'd prepare
  • Business plan / activity proof
  • KYC of proprietor/partners
  • Bank statement (where applicable)
Interest rate and sanction are entirely the lending institution's decision. Apply directly via your bank or udyamimitra.in if you prefer — we only help if you want documentation support.

CGTMSE — Collateral-Free Guarantee

For existing, running businesses only
Eligibility
  • Business vintage of 3+ years
  • Positive net worth
  • Clean CIBIL / credit history
Documents we'd prepare
  • Last 3 years ITR & audited financials
  • Bank statements, GST returns
  • Existing loan/credit history summary
If your business is under 3 years old, CGTMSE is not an option yet — we'll tell you that on the first call, not after you've paid us.

NGO Grants — CSR & NGO Darpan

Section 8 Companies, Trusts, Societies
Eligibility
  • 3+ years operational history
  • Valid 12A and 80G registration
  • Clean audited financials
Documents we'd prepare
  • Trust Deed / Certificate of Incorporation
  • NGO Darpan & CSR-1 registration support
  • 3-year audit report compilation
CSR funding decisions sit with the corporate donor, not with us. We help you become a credible, fully-documented applicant — nothing more.
03

How a file actually moves

01

Free eligibility call

We ask about your business age, turnover, and structure, and tell you honestly which scheme — if any — fits. No fee for this conversation.

02

Document checklist

You receive the exact list for your category. You gather what you have; we tell you what's missing and how to get it.

03

Project Report drafting

We draft the technical and financial projection the bank or KVIC will actually read. This is the core paid service.

04

Portal filing

We submit your application on the relevant government/bank portal and keep a copy of every submission for your records.

05

Interview preparation

If the bank or nodal officer calls you in, we brief you on what's usually asked — the decision is theirs alone.

06

Outcome — either way

If sanctioned, we close the engagement per the agreed fee structure. If not, you keep every document we prepared, at no extra cost.

04

How we charge

Flat fee

Option B
Success-only

No fee unless your loan is actually sanctioned and disbursed. Fee is a pre-agreed percentage of the disbursed amount, stated in the engagement letter.
